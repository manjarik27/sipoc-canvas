How to use this canvas

1. Start with the Humans

Don't start with the process steps. Start by defining who receives the value. What is their pain point? What do they need?

2. Define the Artifacts

Before you figure out how to make it, define what you are making. What tangible thing passes from you to the human?

3. Map the Journey

Now, fill in the middle. Keep it high level (5-7 steps max). Design thinking is about the big picture flow, not the microscopic details.

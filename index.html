Recommended Workflow

1. The Journey (Middle)

First, fill out the 5 center boxes (Steps 1-5). Describe the high-level process flow.

2. The End Goal (Row 5)

Go to the last row. Define the final Artifact and the Human who receives it. This anchors your value.

3. The Start Point (Row 1)

Go to the first row. Define the trigger Ingredient and the Partner who supplies it.

4. Fill the Gaps

Fill in the remaining boxes for each step. What does Step 2 need? What does Step 3 produce?
